    The BeerMore is a Canadian privately owned chain of retail outlets selling local beer and other malt beverages in Oakville, Canada. Since founded in 1990, The BeerMore was formerly the only retailer permitted to sell beer for off-site consumption under Ontario's Liquor Control Act.

    Amendments made to the Liquor Control Act have since allowed for the sale of single and 6 packs of beer at select supermarkets in Ontario. That was done to enhance customer access and convenience. The BeerMore, however, continues to maintain pricing exclusivity in providing consumers discounts on larger packs of beer, along with retail partners, agency stores, combination stores and manufacturer outlets.
